My Reflection

My experience here at St. Edward has been challenging, as nothing in life comes easy. However, it has also been fun and memorable. St. Edward has been a significant part of my life for approximately six years. During my time here, I have met many different kinds of people, made a lot of friends, and even a few best friends who will always be there for me. I have also encountered a variety of teachers, from strict to very kind ones. Some of the teachers I will never forget and will always be thankful for include Sr. Gercov, Sir Spencer, Sr. Dune, Ms. Nicole, Ms. Jo, and many more. Because of these people, I was able to enjoy learning, move forward, overcome challenges, and enjoy my everyday life at school.
